Part 1: Business Operations & Strategy

SLIDE 1.0

What is FreshGuard AI?

🎯

The First Intelligent Freshness Network

FreshGuard AI is a comprehensive enterprise inventory platform designed specifically to eradicate food and product waste in the retail sector.

Instead of relying on random manual checks, we equip ground-floor staff with a high-velocity mobile scanning application that instantly reads barcodes and expiry dates. That data is beamed to a centralized AI cloud that predicts waste, recommends stock transfers, and visualizes immediate financial risks on a real-time command dashboard.
SLIDE 1.1

The Multi-Million Dollar Expiry Challenge

⚠️

The "Manual Audit Trap"

Retailers spend thousands of labor hours cross-checking shelves with clipboards. It's slow, unscalable, and prone to human error—meaning expired stock routinely slips through to consumers, triggering compliance and PR disasters.
💸

Invisible Revenue Bleed

Without proactive visibility, products quietly expire in backrooms. Globally, retail shrinks over $160 Billion annually due to expired perishables. Most managers only realize stock is unsellable after the date has already passed.
SLIDE 1.2

Our Solution Workflow

📱

Scan, Sync, Solve

  • 1. Zero-Friction Capture Workers use standard mobile devices to scan items natively on the shelf. The app extracts product IDs and Expiry Dates simultaneously in milliseconds, without manual typing.
  • 2. Resilient Tracking It logs exactly *where* the item is (Zone, Section, Shelf) and *how many* exist, creating a high-density 4D location map of the store.
  • 3. Proactive Notifications The system alerts HQ 30, 15, and 7 days before items expire, allowing time to mark down prices or transfer stock to high-traffic stores.

Part 2: Technical Architecture

SLIDE 2.0

Ultra-Fast Computer Vision Streams

Google ML Kit + Regex Extraction

Our scanner avoids the lag of native photo-taking by reading raw byte buffers directly from the camera stream using `InputImage.fromBytes`.

The `DateExtractor` parses OCR blocks globally, matching multi-format dates (DD/MM/YY, YYYY-MM-DD), and algorithmically prioritizes future dates to ignore Manufacturer stamps.

Sensor Optimization

We manipulate the Flutter `CameraController` to enforce `ResolutionPreset.high`, resolving auto-focus anomalies on modern devices, while maintaining custom animated `ScannerOverlay` masking via non-blocking `AnimatedBuilder` layers in the UI thread.
SLIDE 2.1

Offline-First Mobile Architecture

Resilient Flutter Engineering

Retail environments often have terrible WiFi. Our Flutter application guarantees zero data loss by utilizing an advanced `DatabaseHelper` local SQLite strategy.
  • Local First Execution Scans instantly write to local tables via optimized batch transactions, providing a zero-latency UI experience regardless of network state.
  • Riverpod Sync Polling A background `SyncService` monitors network status and silently bulk-uploads `[operation: 'ADD', 'UPDATE', 'DELETE']` payloads to the cloud as soon as connection is restored.

SLIDE 2.2

The Node.js Predictive Engine

Cognitive Forecasting Layer

The backend `AIService` utilizes Express.js and Sequelize to calculate stock velocity against upcoming expiration dates.
  • Velocity Formula Sales arrays are aggregated to generate a `30-Day Velocity`. The engine then calculates `Coverage Quotients` (Stock Qty / Velocity).
  • Matrix States Based on coverage ratios and Days-to-Expiry, items are tagged as `Critical Risk`, `Overstocked`, or `Optimized`.

SLIDE 2.3

Enterprise Deployment & Security

Zero-Dependency Packaging

To eliminate server dependency hell in retail HQs, the entire Node.js backend AND compiled React Dashboard are bundled into a single, standalone binary executable via Vercel's `pkg` (`FreshGuard_API_V1.0.7.exe`).

Defense-in-Depth & Real-Time Sync

  • JWT Zero-Trust stateless rotating tokens shield the multi-tenant APIs.
  • Server-Sent Events (SSE): A lightweight `text/event-stream` pipeline broadcasts native `inventory_updated` events direct to the React dashboard, forcing instantaneous charting re-renders without WebSockets.
