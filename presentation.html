01 / OVERVIEW

Project Context

Objective

Develop a centralized system to manage certification validity, renewal reminders, payment tracking, and document management.

Industry Context

Organizations providing international certifications (ISO, Equipment Inspection) requiring efficient tracking of validity and payment cycles.

Current Ecosystem

  • SAP ERP (Backend)
  • Salesforce CRM

Roadmap

  • Phase 1: Standalone System
  • Future: ERP & CRM Integration
02 / CHALLENGES

The Problem

Lack of Centralization

  • No unified platform for certificate validity.
  • Manual notification processes.
  • Fragmented credit payment tracking.
  • Difficult document retrieval.

Business Impact

  • Missed renewal opportunities
  • Delayed customer payments
  • High manual tracking effort
  • Poor lifecycle visibility
